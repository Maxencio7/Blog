LEAN SIX SIGMA METHODOLOGY: A Brief Overview


Lean Six Sigma is a powerful methodology that combines the principles of Lean (focused on efficiency and waste reduction) with the statistical rigor of Six Sigma (aimed at minimizing defects and variations). The goal is to enhance processes, reduce errors, and optimize performance.
Here are the key components of Lean Six Sigma:
  • Define: Clearly articulate the problem, project scope, and
  • Measure: Gather data to understand the current state of the process.
  • Analyze: Identify root causes of issues using statistical tools and techniques.
  • Improve: Implement solutions to address the identified problems.
  • Control: Sustain improvements and prevent regression.
